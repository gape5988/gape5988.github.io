This project allowed us to demonstrate our proficiency in AI algorithms for two player games, such as mancala. I had a great time coding this project and I have future plans to expand the algorithms for other common two player games

SongSeeker

During my sophomore year, I was able to work in a small software team to create a music recommending service from scratch. This project leverages two APIs in specific, both Spotify and LastFM, combining this with clean user interface and database management to make a clean final product to deploy on the internet. This project taught me a lot about working on software in large groups, and also made me quite skeptical when it comes to the use of APIs (since they change frequently).

Link to project

NASA HUNCH Lunar Tabletop Project Prototype

During my senior year of high school, I had the super unique opportunity to work in a small team in a competition hosted by NASA, called HUNCH. We were tasked with designing and build a prototype lunar table that NASA employees evaluated based on its ability to be used on the moon's surface. In competing in this project, I got great firsthand experience of the engineering cycle and how to collaborate in a team setting to meet strict deadlines.
