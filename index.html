Welcome / About Me

My name is Gavin Petruzzi, and I am an extremely versatile Computer Science student currently in my junior year at CU Boulder. I have an extreme passion for both coding and the field of aerospace, but I also love anything to do with machine learning and AI projects. Some of my most significant attribute include my committment, hardworking mindset, and ability to communicate with any and all different personalities. I continue to improve these traits everyday and look forward for new opportunities to grow in other ways too. After a brief time working as a course assistant at CU Boulder, I landed a position at CU's own Laboratory for Atmospheric and Space Physics, where I'm grateful to begin to explore my options in the space industry. I don't know where my career path will take my quite yet, but I'm eager for the experiences to come. Outside of school and work, I love outdoor activites and trying new things. I have a very active lifestyle and a super intense passion for soccer ever since I was really young. I also love music and want to travel the world someday! I want this portfolio to serve as a place where I can showcase the work I've already done and a place where I can detail my upcoming plans.
